The anime expert that will help you to decide!

AskMrOtaku is a recommender system that will recommend you some series based on your preferences.

To get your recommended series you just need to rate at least one anime using the icons.

sentiment_very_dissatisfied  Bad  sentiment_neutral  Neutral  sentiment_very_satisfied  Good

The recommendations are given using three models:

-User Based: series that people similar to you like

-Cluster 1: series with the genres (and other things) that you like

-Cluster 2: series with the main staff that you like
